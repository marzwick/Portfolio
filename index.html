Mara Zwicker

Mara is currently a junior at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, studying Geographic Information Systems, Data Science, and Digital Studies. She has a strong background in geospatial analysis, machine learning, and predictive modeling, with a focus on terrain and environmental forecasting. She worked as a Data Intern at Tiny Earth, where she leverages Python and GIS to enhance scientific research databases. She is also the founder of an AI-driven ocean exploration project, developing machine learning models to predict seafloor and marine features over time. Outside of work, Mara is a Division 1 athlete on the Wisconsin Women's Rowing Team and enjoys exploring the outdoors, whether on the water or hiking new trails
